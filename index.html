Besides my quantum research, I have also huge interests in deep learning and LLM. I have finished several exciting side projects. More coming up in the Projects section.

Currently I am a 6-th year physics PhD at UT Austin. Previously I obtained my B.S. (high honor) with double majors in Physics and Pure Math from the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor.
